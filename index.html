Research Work

Exploring the intersection of computational biology and pharmacology in the context of modern medicine.

Molecular Interactions of ACE Inhibitors with ACE Receptors
(Captopril & Enalapril in COVID-19)

This research investigates the molecular docking interactions of ACE inhibitors — specifically Captopril and Enalapril — with ACE2 receptors, exploring their significance in the context of COVID-19 pathophysiology and potential therapeutic applications. The study provides insight into drug-receptor binding affinity and pharmacological implications for hypertensive patients during viral infection.
